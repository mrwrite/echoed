## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Platform-wide Afrocentric grounding
The platform SHALL define Afrocentric educational grounding as a cross-cutting curricular requirement that can shape all pathways rather than a single cultural or history-only pathway.

#### Scenario: Afrocentric grounding extends beyond heritage pathways
- **WHEN** curriculum architecture is defined for math, science, literacy, economics, engineering, or workforce pathways
- **THEN** the architecture MUST support culturally grounded representation, diaspora relevance, and community-centered pedagogy across those pathways

### Requirement: Representation and pedagogy expectations
The platform SHALL define expectations for African and African-American perspectives, global Black experiences, culturally responsive pedagogy, and community-centered learning in pathway design.

#### Scenario: Cultural grounding remains structural
- **WHEN** pathway-level curricular requirements are documented
- **THEN** representation and pedagogy expectations MUST be expressed as structural curriculum requirements rather than optional enrichment notes
