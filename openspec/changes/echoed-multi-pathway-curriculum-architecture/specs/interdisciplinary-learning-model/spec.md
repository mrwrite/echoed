## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Governed interdisciplinary relationships
The platform SHALL define canonical interdisciplinary relationships among math, science, literacy, history, economics, engineering, arts, entrepreneurship, and civic engagement without creating fragmented curriculum systems.

#### Scenario: Cross-disciplinary links stay canonical
- **WHEN** a pathway or course spans multiple disciplines
- **THEN** the architecture MUST define governed cross-disciplinary relationships instead of duplicating curriculum structures per subject

### Requirement: Interdisciplinary discoverability and integrity
The platform SHALL define how interdisciplinary experiences are discoverable to learners and educators while preserving institutional coherence, pathway ownership, and assessment alignment.

#### Scenario: Interdisciplinary learning does not fragment pathway identity
- **WHEN** a learner participates in curriculum that crosses subject domains
- **THEN** the learner experience MUST remain coherent within one curriculum system and not depend on unrelated parallel pathway models
