## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Multi-pathway learner journey
The platform SHALL define learner discovery, pathway enrollment, multi-pathway participation, pacing flexibility, cohort models, and learner identity expectations for a canonical multi-pathway journey.

#### Scenario: Learners can participate across pathways coherently
- **WHEN** a learner participates in more than one pathway family
- **THEN** the architecture MUST define how that participation remains coherent within one learner journey model rather than separate pathway-specific identity systems

### Requirement: Parent, homeschool, and guidance alignment
The platform SHALL define how parent or homeschool involvement, college and career guidance alignment, and learner support roles fit into the pathway journey architecture.

#### Scenario: Family and guidance roles are institutionally supported
- **WHEN** a pathway supports homeschool or college and career preparation use cases
- **THEN** the architecture MUST specify how support roles participate without weakening governed learner or educator authority
