## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical lesson and unit progression blueprint
The platform SHALL define lesson sequencing philosophy, unit progression philosophy, prerequisite boundaries, learner continuation semantics, educator intervention points, unavailable-state expectations, and mastery gating philosophy for flagship learning experiences.

#### Scenario: Lesson and unit flow remain institutionally coherent
- **WHEN** a learner moves through a flagship unit
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST define how lessons sequence, when progression pauses, and where intervention or continuation decisions become visible

### Requirement: Governed availability and continuation semantics
The flagship blueprint SHALL preserve governed unavailable-state expectations and continuity semantics without changing the current governed delivery architecture.

#### Scenario: Availability remains aligned to governed delivery
- **WHEN** a lesson is not yet available or a learner needs remediation before continuing
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST describe the instructional expectation while preserving current governed delivery semantics as the runtime authority
