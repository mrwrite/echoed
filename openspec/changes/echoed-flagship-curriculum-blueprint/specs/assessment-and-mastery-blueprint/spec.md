## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Flagship assessment and mastery blueprint
The platform SHALL define how formative assessment, summative assessment, reflection or journal evidence, discussion evidence, project-based evidence, competency alignment, remediation expectations, mastery thresholds, and enrichment expectations operate within the flagship blueprint.

#### Scenario: Mastery evidence stays grounded in current foundations
- **WHEN** the flagship blueprint defines how learners demonstrate understanding
- **THEN** it MUST operationalize the existing assessment and mastery foundation rather than creating a flagship-specific assessment engine

### Requirement: Remediation and enrichment expectations
The flagship blueprint SHALL define how mastery signals lead to remediation or enrichment expectations while preserving current progression and assessment runtime semantics until explicitly extended.

#### Scenario: Learner next steps remain instructionally explicit
- **WHEN** a learner demonstrates incomplete or advanced understanding
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST define the expected remediation or enrichment response without changing current backend progression behavior by implication
