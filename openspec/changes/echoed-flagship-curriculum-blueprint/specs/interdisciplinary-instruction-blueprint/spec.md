## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Flagship interdisciplinary pathway model
The platform SHALL use “Introduction to Africa” for K-5 as the canonical interdisciplinary proving-ground pathway for defining how geography, literacy, science, arts, storytelling, music, ecosystems, and civic understanding interconnect inside one governed learning experience.

#### Scenario: Interdisciplinary learning is modeled concretely
- **WHEN** the flagship blueprint defines interdisciplinary instruction
- **THEN** it MUST show how multiple domains interconnect naturally within one pathway rather than appearing as separate subject fragments

### Requirement: Interdisciplinary coherence remains governed
The flagship interdisciplinary model SHALL preserve curricular coherence, pathway identity, and assessment alignment without introducing a parallel curriculum graph or duplicated lesson system.

#### Scenario: Cross-disciplinary learning stays inside one governed pathway
- **WHEN** the flagship pathway connects multiple subject domains
- **THEN** those connections MUST remain governed within the existing learning architecture
