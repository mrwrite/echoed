## Requirements
### Requirement: Runtime support remains additive to current architecture
The platform SHALL implement runtime remediation, enrichment, reinforcement, and educator-awareness behavior as additive architecture on top of governed delivery, assessment/mastery foundations, auth/session authority, reporting compatibility, and premium UX primitives.

#### Scenario: No parallel runtime engine is introduced
- **WHEN** runtime support capabilities are implemented
- **THEN** they MUST preserve Angular architecture, Tailwind strategy, governed progression authority, and current assessment/mastery foundations
- **AND** they MUST NOT create duplicated progression logic, duplicated mastery systems, or fragmented learner-state engines

### Requirement: Reporting compatibility is preserved
The platform SHALL keep runtime support semantics compatible with current and future reporting read models without changing certification behavior by implication.

#### Scenario: Runtime support remains explainable in reporting
- **WHEN** runtime remediation or enrichment states are represented conceptually
- **THEN** they MUST remain derivable from authoritative learner evidence and governed state
- **AND** they MUST NOT change certification or transcript semantics implicitly
