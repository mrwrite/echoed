## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical educator facilitation blueprint
The platform SHALL define educator preparation expectations, live facilitation workflows, intervention expectations, small-group usage, classroom pacing, homeschool facilitation, asynchronous facilitation, assignment visibility, and learner support expectations for flagship instruction.

#### Scenario: Educators can facilitate the flagship pathway across contexts
- **WHEN** the flagship blueprint is applied in classroom, homeschool, or asynchronous settings
- **THEN** it MUST define how educator roles adapt without requiring separate platform models

### Requirement: Educator workflows remain aligned to platform operations
The educator facilitation blueprint SHALL align with existing educator operational workflows and visibility foundations rather than introducing a second facilitation system.

#### Scenario: Operational integrity is preserved
- **WHEN** educator-facing facilitation expectations are defined
- **THEN** they MUST build on the existing educator operational model and preserve governed visibility boundaries
