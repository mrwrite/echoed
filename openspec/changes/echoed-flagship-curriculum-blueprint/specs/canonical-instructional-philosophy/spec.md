## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical instructional philosophy
The platform SHALL define a canonical instructional philosophy for EchoEd that covers culturally responsive pedagogy, learner agency, immersion, pacing, cognitive load, mastery-first learning, educator-guided facilitation, experiential learning, delight, emotional engagement, and accessibility.

#### Scenario: Future pathways inherit one instructional standard
- **WHEN** future pathways are planned from the flagship blueprint
- **THEN** they MUST inherit one coherent instructional philosophy rather than inventing separate pedagogical models

### Requirement: Instructional philosophy remains Afrocentrically grounded
The flagship instructional philosophy SHALL express Afrocentric grounding, cultural relevance, representation, and community-centered learning as platform-wide expectations rather than pathway-local ornamentation.

#### Scenario: Cultural grounding remains structural
- **WHEN** the flagship blueprint is used as the proving-ground for future instruction
- **THEN** cultural grounding MUST shape the learning philosophy itself and not appear only in heritage-specific content moments
