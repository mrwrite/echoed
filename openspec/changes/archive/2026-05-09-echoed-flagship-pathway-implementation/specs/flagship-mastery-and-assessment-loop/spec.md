## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Flagship mastery and assessment loop
The system SHALL operationalize the flagship pathway’s mastery loop through formative checks, lightweight summative assessments, reflection evidence, discussion evidence, competency alignment, remediation paths, and enrichment paths.

#### Scenario: Mastery is demonstrated through bounded evidence
- **WHEN** a learner progresses through the flagship pathway
- **THEN** the pathway MUST collect and interpret bounded instructional evidence through the existing assessment/mastery foundation

### Requirement: Assessment compatibility is preserved
The flagship mastery loop SHALL preserve institutional assessment architecture, governed progression integrity, and reporting compatibility without introducing a new assessment engine.

#### Scenario: Flagship mastery remains institutionally aligned
- **WHEN** the flagship pathway implements its mastery loop
- **THEN** assessment behavior MUST remain compatible with the current assessment foundation and future reporting expectations
