## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Pathway-level mastery semantics
The platform SHALL define pathway-level mastery semantics, interdisciplinary competency expectations, remediation philosophy, and mastery portability in a way that extends the institutional assessment foundation without creating a new assessment engine.

#### Scenario: Pathway mastery builds on current foundations
- **WHEN** mastery requirements are defined for curriculum pathways
- **THEN** those requirements MUST build on the existing assessment and mastery foundation rather than inventing pathway-local grading semantics

### Requirement: Evidence and credentialing boundaries
The platform SHALL define academic evidence expectations, credentialing boundaries, and progression-integrity expectations for pathway participation while preserving current certification behavior until explicitly extended.

#### Scenario: Credentialing remains institutionally coherent
- **WHEN** pathway completion or mastery is evaluated conceptually
- **THEN** evidence expectations and credentialing boundaries MUST be explicit and MUST NOT change existing certification semantics by implication alone
