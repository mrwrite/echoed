## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Family and community integration philosophy
The platform SHALL define parent visibility philosophy, family discussion prompts, offline reinforcement expectations, community-centered learning expectations, optional home extensions, and homeschool adaptation expectations for flagship instruction.

#### Scenario: Family support reinforces learning coherently
- **WHEN** the flagship pathway is used in homeschool or family-supported settings
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST define how family and community participation reinforce learning without becoming hidden mandatory platform work

### Requirement: Community-centered learning remains bounded
The family and community integration model SHALL encourage community-centered learning while preserving learner accessibility, educator authority, and institutional coherence.

#### Scenario: Community integration does not create off-platform fragmentation
- **WHEN** community-centered reinforcement is incorporated into the flagship blueprint
- **THEN** it MUST remain aligned to the governed instructional experience and not split learning into disconnected tracks
