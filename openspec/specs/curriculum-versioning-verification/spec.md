## Requirements
### Requirement: Safe publishing must preserve learner continuity and evidence integrity
EchoEd SHALL define verification expectations for curriculum revision and republish behavior before broader workflow implementation.

#### Scenario: Verification covers learner continuity
- **GIVEN** a published curriculum revision or archival event occurs conceptually
- **WHEN** verification expectations are defined
- **THEN** they SHALL cover learner continuity, progress safety, assessment evidence preservation, mastery integrity, certification preservation, and runtime support evidence continuity

#### Scenario: Verification guards against progression corruption
- **GIVEN** governed learner delivery and progression remain authoritative
- **WHEN** safe publishing behavior is implemented in future phases
- **THEN** regression expectations SHALL confirm that curriculum revision does not corrupt learner progression or historical interpretation
