## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Focused flagship regression coverage
The system SHALL include focused regression coverage for flagship lesson flow, governed progression, assessment/mastery flow, educator visibility, responsive learner experience, accessibility expectations, unavailable-state handling, and continuation continuity.

#### Scenario: Flagship slice remains stable as a canonical reference
- **WHEN** the flagship pathway implementation evolves
- **THEN** regression coverage MUST verify that the flagship slice continues to reflect the intended canonical learner, educator, and mastery experience

### Requirement: Verification preserves institutional integrity
The flagship verification strategy SHALL confirm that no parallel learning system, fragmented progression path, or incompatible assessment flow is introduced during implementation.

#### Scenario: Canonical platform authority remains intact
- **WHEN** the flagship implementation is tested and reviewed
- **THEN** verification MUST confirm that governed delivery, progression integrity, and assessment compatibility remain intact
