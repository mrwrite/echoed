## Requirements
### Requirement: Bounded runtime enrichment continuation
The platform SHALL define bounded runtime enrichment continuation that offers optional deeper exploration while preserving the current governed pathway and without creating a separate enrichment engine.

#### Scenario: Enrichment remains optional and governed
- **WHEN** a learner is eligible for extension or curiosity-driven exploration
- **THEN** the platform MUST express enrichment as an optional continuation state or bounded extension
- **AND** it MUST NOT replace the canonical governed sequence for the learner

### Requirement: Culturally grounded extension support
The platform SHALL define culturally grounded enrichment prompts and extension activities that build on existing lesson and flagship pathway foundations.

#### Scenario: Enrichment deepens without fragmenting the pathway
- **WHEN** deeper-learning opportunities are offered
- **THEN** they MUST remain connected to current objectives, pathway identity, and institutional runtime boundaries
- **AND** they MUST NOT create uncontrolled branching complexity
