## Requirements
### Requirement: Flagship educator facilitation implementation
The system SHALL implement educator lesson facilitation support, pacing visibility, learner-progress visibility, intervention awareness, assignment visibility, discussion facilitation guidance, and classroom or homeschool adaptation support for the flagship pathway.

#### Scenario: Educators can facilitate the flagship pathway coherently
- **WHEN** educators use the flagship pathway in classroom or homeschool contexts
- **THEN** they MUST receive bounded facilitation and visibility support without requiring a separate educator platform

### Requirement: Educator tooling remains bounded
The flagship educator slice SHALL avoid enterprise LMS complexity, heavy grading systems, and parallel educator tooling.

#### Scenario: Educator support stays within platform strategy
- **WHEN** educator implementation choices are made for the flagship pathway
- **THEN** they MUST remain lightweight, instructional, and aligned to existing educator operational workflows
