## Requirements
### Requirement: Family and community implementation slice
The system SHALL implement family discussion prompts, optional home reinforcement activities, culturally grounded community prompts, and lightweight printable or offline extension support where reasonable for the flagship pathway.

#### Scenario: Family reinforcement remains optional and coherent
- **WHEN** family and community extensions are offered in the flagship pathway
- **THEN** they MUST reinforce learning without becoming a separate parent platform or hidden requirement for success

### Requirement: Family/community support remains lightweight
The flagship family/community slice SHALL avoid separate parent platform architecture and complex messaging systems.

#### Scenario: Family integration stays bounded
- **WHEN** family/community support is implemented
- **THEN** it MUST remain lightweight and compatible with the canonical learner and educator experience
