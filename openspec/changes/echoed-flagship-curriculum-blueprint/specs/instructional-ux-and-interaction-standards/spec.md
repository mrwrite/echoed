## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical instructional UX rhythm
The platform SHALL define instructional UX and interaction standards for page rhythm, content density, media usage, motion boundaries, responsive behavior, notification philosophy, blocked or unavailable-state tone, and success or reward tone inside flagship learning experiences.

#### Scenario: Instructional surfaces feel coherent and premium
- **WHEN** learners or educators interact with flagship lesson and assessment flows
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST define a coherent instructional UX rhythm aligned to the premium learning experience foundations

### Requirement: Instructional UX remains bounded and non-fragmented
The instructional UX blueprint SHALL extend existing premium UX standards without introducing fragmented paradigms, excessive LMS-style complexity, or a separate instructional shell model.

#### Scenario: UX standards preserve architectural continuity
- **WHEN** instructional UX expectations are documented
- **THEN** they MUST preserve current shell and state-system foundations while clarifying the learning-specific interaction standard
