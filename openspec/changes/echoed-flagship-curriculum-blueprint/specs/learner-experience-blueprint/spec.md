## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical learner emotional and motivational journey
The platform SHALL define the learner emotional journey, motivation and reward philosophy, progress visibility, identity and belonging expectations, culturally affirming interaction philosophy, accessibility expectations, continuity expectations, and mobile or low-bandwidth experience expectations for flagship learning.

#### Scenario: Learner experience remains coherent across sessions
- **WHEN** a learner returns to the flagship pathway across multiple sessions
- **THEN** the blueprint MUST define a continuous, culturally affirming, and mastery-oriented experience rather than disconnected lesson events

### Requirement: Accessibility and belonging remain first-class
The flagship learner blueprint SHALL treat accessibility, belonging, and culturally affirming interaction as core experience requirements rather than later UX refinements.

#### Scenario: Inclusion is part of the instructional model
- **WHEN** learner experience expectations are defined
- **THEN** accessibility and belonging requirements MUST be explicit within the flagship blueprint itself
