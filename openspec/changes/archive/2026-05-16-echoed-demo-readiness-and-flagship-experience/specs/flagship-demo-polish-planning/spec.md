# Flagship Demo Polish Planning

## Purpose

Capture the bounded polish planning needed to turn the flagship EchoEd demo into a presentation-ready experience without redesigning the platform in this change.

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Bounded polish planning

The change SHALL identify the highest-value presentation polish opportunities for the flagship demo without implementing a broad UI redesign.

#### Scenario: Demo polish plan is documented

- **WHEN** the change artifacts are reviewed
- **THEN** they identify bounded next-step polish work for student, teacher, and admin demo flows
- **AND** the planning stays scoped to presentation readiness rather than new platform systems

### Requirement: No workflow expansion in polish planning

The polish plan SHALL avoid introducing new workflow systems under the guise of demo improvement.

#### Scenario: Demo polish scope is evaluated

- **WHEN** proposed polish work is reviewed
- **THEN** it does not require new governance engines, versioning systems, orchestration workflows, classroom sync systems, or analytics platforms
- **AND** it remains aligned to flagship experience polish only
