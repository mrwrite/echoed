## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Governed educator authoring boundaries
The platform SHALL define educator authoring boundaries, pathway ownership responsibilities, curriculum governance, and approval or review workflows for institutional curriculum development.

#### Scenario: Authoring remains governed
- **WHEN** educators or curriculum leads contribute to pathway design or adaptation
- **THEN** the architecture MUST define who can author, review, approve, publish, and adapt curriculum within governed boundaries

### Requirement: Organization-level adaptation boundaries
The platform SHALL define how organizations can regionally or culturally adapt canonical pathways without creating uncontrolled forks or parallel curriculum systems.

#### Scenario: Customization stays inside platform governance
- **WHEN** an organization needs local adaptation for region, culture, or institutional context
- **THEN** the adaptation rules MUST preserve canonical governance and publication integrity
