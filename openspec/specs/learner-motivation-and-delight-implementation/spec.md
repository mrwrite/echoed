## Requirements
### Requirement: Flagship learner motivation and delight
The system SHALL implement completion celebration, progress visibility, culturally affirming interaction patterns, emotionally supportive UX, and lightweight reward or progress reinforcement for the flagship pathway.

#### Scenario: Motivation supports learning without manipulation
- **WHEN** the learner completes meaningful progress milestones in the flagship pathway
- **THEN** the system MUST reinforce progress in a culturally affirming and emotionally supportive way without manipulative gamification

### Requirement: Reward patterns remain bounded
The flagship pathway SHALL avoid dopamine-loop mechanics and excessive badges or points systems.

#### Scenario: Delight remains institutionally appropriate
- **WHEN** motivation and celebration patterns are introduced
- **THEN** they MUST remain lightweight and academically credible
