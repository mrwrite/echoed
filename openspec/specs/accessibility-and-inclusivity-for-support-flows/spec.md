## Requirements
### Requirement: Accessible and low-cognitive-load runtime support
The platform SHALL define remediation, retry, enrichment, and reinforcement patterns that remain accessible, low-cognitive-load, and mobile-friendly.

#### Scenario: Support flows remain inclusive
- **WHEN** runtime support guidance is shown to learners
- **THEN** the interactions, copy, spacing, and transitions MUST remain accessible and readable across devices
- **AND** they MUST support low-friction recovery for younger learners and diverse learning contexts

### Requirement: Culturally affirming support tone
The platform SHALL define runtime support tone and interaction guidance that remains culturally affirming and aligned to EchoEd’s flagship educational identity.

#### Scenario: Support messaging reflects platform identity
- **WHEN** encouragement, remediation, or enrichment messaging is presented
- **THEN** it MUST reinforce care, belonging, curiosity, and dignity
- **AND** it MUST remain compatible with Afrocentric grounding and premium UX standards
