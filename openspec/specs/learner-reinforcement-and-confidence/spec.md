## Requirements
### Requirement: Emotionally supportive learner reinforcement
The platform SHALL define runtime reinforcement semantics that encourage learners, preserve confidence, and celebrate progress without manipulative gamification.

#### Scenario: Retry and reinforcement preserve dignity
- **WHEN** a learner receives runtime feedback after struggle, retry, or success
- **THEN** the platform MUST use encouraging, confidence-preserving, culturally affirming language
- **AND** it MUST NOT rely on streaks, exploitative reward loops, or excessive points systems

### Requirement: Bounded celebration and momentum continuity
The platform SHALL define bounded celebration moments and momentum-preserving reinforcement that support continuation rather than distracting from learning.

#### Scenario: Celebration supports continuity
- **WHEN** a learner completes a support step, remediation step, or enrichment step
- **THEN** reinforcement MUST encourage the next meaningful action
- **AND** it MUST remain compatible with the premium UX foundation and current continuation flow
