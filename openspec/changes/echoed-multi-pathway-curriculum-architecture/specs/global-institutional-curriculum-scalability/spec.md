## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Global and institutional scalability boundaries
The platform SHALL define how the curriculum architecture supports international readiness, institutional partnerships, homeschool cooperatives, NGO or community-center usage, and educator scaling without changing the canonical learning system.

#### Scenario: Deployment models remain inside one architecture
- **WHEN** EchoEd is evaluated for different institutional or community deployment models
- **THEN** the architecture MUST describe those models as supported configurations of one system rather than separate curriculum platforms

### Requirement: Localization and low-bandwidth philosophy
The platform SHALL define localization philosophy, low-bandwidth and offline-minded expectations, and global readiness boundaries for future implementation phases.

#### Scenario: Global readiness is planned early
- **WHEN** curriculum architecture is evaluated for international or low-connectivity environments
- **THEN** the design MUST include explicit guidance for localization and bandwidth-aware delivery expectations
