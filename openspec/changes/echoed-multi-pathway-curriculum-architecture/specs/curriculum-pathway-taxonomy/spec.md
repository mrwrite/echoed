## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Canonical pathway taxonomy
The platform SHALL define one canonical curriculum pathway taxonomy that organizes flagship cultural pathways, core academics, STEM, literacy, financial education, entrepreneurship, workforce readiness, homeschool, GED or adult education, higher-ed preparation, and enrichment pathways within the same institutional learning system.

#### Scenario: Pathway taxonomy covers multiple institutional use cases
- **WHEN** curriculum architecture is defined for long-term platform planning
- **THEN** the taxonomy MUST represent both flagship Afrocentric learning pathways and academic or workforce-oriented pathways without requiring separate curriculum systems

### Requirement: Pathway governance and discoverability
The platform SHALL define pathway ownership, governance boundaries, subject hierarchy relationships, interdisciplinary discoverability principles, and publication philosophy for every curriculum pathway family.

#### Scenario: Pathway families remain governable and discoverable
- **WHEN** institutions or curriculum leaders evaluate how pathways are organized
- **THEN** each pathway family MUST have explicit governance expectations and discoverability rules that prevent ad hoc or hidden curriculum branches
